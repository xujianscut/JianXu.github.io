A generative-modeling framework for **quantum pure-state ensembles**. The
forward process is formulated as a Riemannian diffusion on complex
projective space (with the Fubini–Study metric) realised by a stochastic
Schrödinger equation, and the reverse-time SDE samples new states with the
target ensemble statistics — observable moments and entanglement measures
included. We discuss applications to **quantum machine learning** via
ensemble-level data augmentation.
